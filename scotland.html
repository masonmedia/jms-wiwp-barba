September 22-28, 2019 · The Dunskey Castle · Wigtownshire, Scotland

Scotland

with Janice Mason Steeves and Rebecca Crowell

Location

This is a small workshop with two instructors, each bringing their personal interests and experience to their teaching. Our focus will be on experiencing the land and translating that response into abstract paintings using water-based media.

Details

Dunskey is a historically listed Edwardian private home built in 1900 on 2000 acres of ocean-front land. The Castle is superbly maintained and retains all of its original features including the majority of furniture and soft furnishings. There are miles of walking trails on this property, as well as a maze and two lakes.

Schedule + Accomodation

The interior features Doric columns in the elegant drawing room, Baronnial stone fireplace in the hushed and grand hall, and lovely small details such as original Delft blue tiles in the Dutch kitchen. The Castle sleeps up to thirty six guests and comes with four grand reception rooms, two kitchens and four other smaller sitting and activity rooms.
