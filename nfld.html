Workshops in Wild Places:

Newfoundland.

June 2-7, 2019 · The Doctor’s House · Green’s Harbour Newfoundland.Read more about the workshop below.

Location

Running from June 2-7, 2019 this workshop takes place at The Doctor's House, a small luxury inn resting on the shores of Trinity Bay, Newfoundland. This Tudor-style Mansion rests on 100 forested acres and features a gourmet restaurant, spa, English-inspired gardens, breathtaking views, and luxury accommodation. The workshop will be held in The Barn, a wide open space with a panoramaic view of the land and the sea beyond. There are trails to walk, and the opportunity in the evening for a campfire overlooking the sea.

Details

Join me for a week-long immersion into the landscape of Newfoundland. Our focus will be on experiencing various ways of connecting to this unique landcape, including meditative walking, observing, contemplating, sketching and journaling. We'll aim to create abstract responses to the land using cold wax medium and oil. In addition to studio time, there will be opportunities to experience the outdoors and an optional boat tour on our day off where we hope to see Icebergs and Whales. In the studio, the focus will be on creating an abstract interpretation of your response to the land. There will be time to share your work, thoughts, and writings, along with a group critique/sharing at the end of the week with discussion and suggestions about how to carry your work forward once you return home.

Info + Signup

The workshop lasts 6 days/7 nights, with arrival the night before, and departure on the morning after. Workshop hours are 9am to 4pm. There will be a free day mid-week for personal time, and an optional boat tour. I am limiting the group to 8 to ensure plenty of one-on-one interaction.
